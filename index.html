    Opened in October 2015, Butterwood Bake Consortium is a dessert cafe located in the Lawrenceville neighbourhood of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The bakery features cake and pie by the slice, custom whole cakes, as well as local KLVN coffee and espresso! They bake with organic Daisy Flour, Wholesome Sweeteners Organic Evaporated Cane Juice, and local, organic eggs. They have been featured in TribLIVE, The Pittsburgh 100, and Discover the Burgh.
